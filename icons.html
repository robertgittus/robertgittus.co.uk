OSX icon bundle and framework

OSX, Icons, Digital, Open Source.

Free open-source OS X bundle and framework gives Apple Mac OS X users the choice to personalise their desktop. The project became an international collaboration with numerous Reddit users designing uniformed icons which were free, open-source and editable. Within a few days the free icons bundle had more than 500 icons.
