Moka.pot

Product, Manufacturing, Coffee-concept

A minimal and contemporary stainless-steel design with influences from Japanese traditions and their Yokode teapots. The shape of the handle increases the efficiency of pouring multiple cups of coffee ensuring that every drop is poured.

The Moka pot would be manufactured from 301 stainless steel (commonly used in 97.040 ISO) due its high strength and excellent corrosion resistance. A high temperature enamel gloss finish will reduce heat dissipation and provide a longer life span.
