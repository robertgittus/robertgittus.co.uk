Meriel Skiing Resort

Brand Identity, Graphics, Advertisement.

A simple brochure insert design for the branding & identity of the French Alps ski resort ‘Méribel’. The minimalistic mountain design ensures that the company’s brand is easily recognised and its facilities are effectively communicated to its customers. Brochure inserts are a successful marketing tool for companies.

The use of a simple colour palette and infographic techniques provides clear functionality and user interaction which is concise and aesthetically attractive. The brochure insert consists of ‘a typical first day’ guide and a simplified map of the skiing routes.
